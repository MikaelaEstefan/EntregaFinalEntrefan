Expense Tracker

Welcome to the Expense Tracker! This simple tool helps you keep track of your expenses by allowing you to add categories and record individual expenses.

Categories

Start by adding categories that represent different types of expenses. To add a category, enter the name in the "Add Category" field and click the "Add" button. The new category will be displayed in the list below, and you can select it when adding expenses.

Expenses

To add an expense, select a category from the dropdown list, enter the amount in the "Expense Amount" field, and click the "Add Expense" button. The new expense will be added to the list below, showing the category, date, and amount.

Generate Report

Once you have added categories and expenses, you can generate a report to see the total amount spent in each category. Click the "Generate Report" button, and a pie chart will be displayed below, showing the distribution of expenses across categories.
