Welcome to your new Expense Tracker!

Getting Started

To get started, open the Expense Tracker by clicking on the Report link in the navbar.

Adding Categories

On the Report page, you will see a form to add new categories.

To add a new one, simply enter the name of the category in the Add a category input field and click on the Add Category button.

The new category will be added to the list of categories, and you will see a success message at the bottom of the page.

Adding Expenses

To add a new expense, select the category of the expense from the Category dropdown, enter the amount of the expense in the Amount input field, and click on the Add Expense button.

The new expense will be added to the list of expenses for the selected category, and you will see a success message at the bottom of the page.

Generating a Report

To generate a report of your expenses, click on the Add Expense button.

You will see a summary of your expenses for each category.
